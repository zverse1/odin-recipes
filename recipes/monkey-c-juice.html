Monkey C Juice

Description

A two ingredient, one step Vitamin C and potassium boost. Recipe from All Recipes

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces freshly-squeezed orange juice
  • 1 medium banana, peeled and quartered

Steps

  1. Place juice and banana in blender. Blend on high until smooth and frothy. about 30 seconds. Serve immediately.
